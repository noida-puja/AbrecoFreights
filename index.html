The Vision

From a single funnel of fire,
a hundred companies.

Abreco Group is a 16-year-old conglomerate founded in Jebel Ali Free Zone, Dubai, by Mohammed Shaji. What began in 2010 as a single freight desk has grown into 14 industries and over 100 operating companies — spanning Freight, Transport, Energy, Banking, Hospitality, Retail, Education, and more.

The mark you see above is the contract behind every one of those companies. Across UAE, Oman, and India, from Jebel Ali to Cochin to Bengaluru, the same 'A' opens the door.

The founding vertical — Abreco Freight — still runs the engine. Eight offices. A hundred countries. Fifty thousand shipments a year. ISO 9001:2015 quality-certified, WCAworld Gold Medallion-protected, and answering the phone.
