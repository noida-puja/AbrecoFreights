The Founding Fire

The crimson sweep — the urgency that gets a truck on the road at 4 a.m. The original Abreco vertical: Freight, since 2010.

The Body of Fire

Fluid, not blocky. A sweep, not a stamp — because every shipment is a fresh negotiation between cost, time, and care.

The Forward Foot

The mark does not stand — it leans forward like a runner mid-stride. Diagonals carry energy. Standing still is losing.

2

The Word

Above the fire, the blue rises. Maersk's tradition since 1886: stability, depth, professionalism. The contract you sign on.

4

Two-Toned Depth

Navy outside. Sky inside. The boardroom and the bay door, speaking the same colour in two octaves.

6

The Hidden 'A'

Between the colours, a letter forms. For Abreco — Ambition, Accountability, Assurance. A FedEx-arrow reveal.
